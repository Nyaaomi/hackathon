What is a Stoop Sale

A stoop sale is a quintessential Brooklyn experience where neighbors gather on their stoops (porches) to sell second-hand goods ranging from vintage finds to household items. It's a vibrant community event where you can discover unique treasures while chatting with locals. A stoop sale is a garage sale but better because it's not just about transactions; it's about connecting with your community and giving new life to pre-loved items in a lively and social atmosphere.
